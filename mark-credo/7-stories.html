7 Stories to Read and Discuss

These 7 readings go right to the heart of Mark’s message about Jesus. They are a brilliant way to meet the real Jesus, and often address the question, ‘Who is this man?’ They are great stories to read and discuss in your Trio, or with a friend. 5 key questions are provided as an easy and helpful guide for your discussion.

1. The Beginning - Mark 1.1-20
2. Dr Jesus - Mark 2.1-17
3. Faith vs Fear - Mark 4:35-5:20
4. Jesus Is _____ - Mark 8:27-38
5. First or Last? - Mark 10:17-38
6. The End - Mark 15.1-15 and 25-39
7. A New Beginning - Mark 15.40-16.8
